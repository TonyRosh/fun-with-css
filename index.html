I've been spending my days studying the ways of web development. Learning the ins and outs of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and using them to bring webpages to life, like the one you're visiting right now!


Using apps like Mimo, Programming Hub, and SoloLearn really helped me start this adventure, but I am now enrolled at Flatiron School and I can't wait to fully immerse myself in the world of coding!


I hope you enjoy that little video of my pup! Her name is Blanca, and (when I'm not studying code) she is my world!

My journey into software engineering started in January of 2021. I was in the restaurant industry for 15 years until the pandemic caused my restaurant to close. I wasn't sure I wanted to find another food service job, and I had some family who had recently completed a coding boot camp, much like Flatiron School. They convinced me that it was a path I needed to follow, so I downloaded a few coding apps to see what it was like. The more I dove into those apps, the more I enjoyed the process and I eventually decided this was something I wanted to pursue! I did some research and found Flatiron School and decided to apply. My courses start on April 26th, and I am very excited to step into the world of coding!


When I'm not coding or playing with my dog, you can usually catch me producing music or enjoying a spooky horror film. If you are into EDM (more specifically, dubstep) then feel free to give my music a listen on SoundCloud here: Snax! If you have horror movie suggestions, I am all ears!


Denver, Colorado has been home to me my whole life, but I recently also bought property the beautiful city of Puerto Vallarta, Jalisco, Mexico! That has been my favorite place in the world to vacation to for years, and I am thrilled to call it my part time home now! I will be renting my condo when I'm not there, so if you're interested in taking a vacation to Mexico, feel free to reach out for details!!
